Rent Prices in the United States

by Adriana DePalma

Interested in the United States rental market? As of January 2021, the overall average of a rental unit (of any size) reached $1,600/month. This tool allows for exploration of the ZORI* price in major cities across the US from 2014-2021.
Using the exploration tool below, you can view the data on a year-to-year basis, and note overtime how many cities rental units are priced well above the national average.

*Zillow Observed Rent Index (ZORI): A smoothed measure of the typical observed market rate rent across a given region. ZORI is a repeat-rent index that is weighted to the rental housing stock to ensure representativeness across the entire market, not just those homes currently listed for-rent.

The index is dollar-denominated by computing the mean of listed rents that fall into the 40th to 60th percentile range for all homes and apartments in a given region, which is once again weighted to reflect the rental housing stock.
